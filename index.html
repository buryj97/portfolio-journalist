Julia Benarrous

Editorial Producer

Currently an Editorial Producer at the VR documentary production studio TARGO in Paris, I am a story-oriented, people-driven professional.

Fluent in French, English, and Spanish, I have experience in writing, content production, research, and strategy. After graduating from Sciences Po Paris with a master's degree in Journalism, I joined AFP's team in the United States as the Southwest text & video correspondent.

I have dabbled in copywriting and comms, content strategy, and publishing. I specialize in tech + culture and harbor a love for innovative formats & storytelling.

Current interests include the environment, choir, and calendars.
