Julia Benarrous

Editorial Producer

Currently an Editorial Producer at the VR documentary production studio TARGO in Paris, I am a story-oriented, people-driven professional interested in film and show production.

Fluent in French, English, and Spanish, I have experience in writing, production, research, licensing, and distribution. I am a skilled planner, organizer, team player, a curious cat, and an observant conversationalist. After graduating from Sciences Po Paris with a master's degree in Journalism, I joined AFP's team in the United States as the Southwest text & video correspondent. I also dabbled in copywriting and comms, content strategy, and publishing. I have been working at TARGO since October 2021.

I specialize in tech & culture and harbor a love for innovative formats & storytelling.

Current interests include composting, choral singing, and barefoot shoes.

Recent Visual Projects

JFK MEMENTO (2023)

Editorial Producer


Documentary Feature
Release November 2023

Sixty years after President Kennedy's assassination, dive into the archives and the testimonies of the last living witnesses of the case to travel back in time thanks to the radically new medium of virtual reality.


Skills: Fact-checking, research, licensing, distribution, writing, planning, budgeting, project management, production


Licensing: Managed the archive licensing process and its budget. Created a massive database of thousands of archival items from the 1960s, located them in the highest quality and found the current owners of these sixty-year-old photos and videos. Also in charge of the freelance workers' envelope and briefing process.

Fact-checking: Selected the correct input of information sent to the 3D team before 3D environment reconstruction and ensure historical accuracy throughout the production period, with help from the Sixth Floor Museum at Dealey Plaza's collections director.

Distribution: Prepared the press kit submitted to journalists and festivals, which included press releases, visual assets and accessibility documents. Participated in the 2023 SXSW festival in Austin, Texas, where JFK Memento Part 1 won the XR Competition Audience Award, as part of the delegation sent by Targo.

Pre-production: Researched characters, located them and conducted pre-interviews before writing scripts to help feed the main scenario. Assisted the director in writing the main scenario.

BEHIND THE DISH (2022)

Editorial Producer


VR Docu-series
Released

Behind the Dish is a virtual reality docuseries on gastronomy and female chefs, filmed in VR 360° and macro stereo 3D.


Skills: Production, project management, research, writing, planning, video capture, budgeting, social media, contracts


Pre-production: Researched food-related topics and characters, conducted pre-interviews with chefs from several countries, and edited script drafts written by the director.

Production: Planned several week-long filming trips to Japan and the USA with the producer, captured 360° videos and sound for each episode of the series on the ground, and was responsible for a budget when hiring and briefing freelancers.

Release: Drew up the release plan of the series and the copy on social media, handled the publishing process on Meta Quest TV and oversaw PR efforts and media outreach. Submitted the series to dozens of festivals throughout the year and responded to interviews on the project.
